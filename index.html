Instructions:

  • Select "Add Node Mode" and click on the canvas to add nodes
  • In Add Node Mode, you can also drag existing nodes to reposition them
  • Select "Add Edge Mode" and click two nodes to connect them
  • Select "Delete Mode" and click nodes/edges to remove them
  • Isolated nodes (no connections) are shown in gray with dashed borders
  • Use "Auto-Layout Components" to organize disconnected parts
  • Click "Calculate Eigenvalues" to compute the Laplacian eigenvalues

Visual Indicators:

  • ● Connected nodes (dark blue-gray)
  • ● Isolated nodes (light gray, dashed border)
  • ● Selected node (blue, thick border)
  • Directed edges show arrows, undirected edges are simple lines
